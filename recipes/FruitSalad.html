Fruit Salad

This bowl is a savory heaven of sorts.

Fruit salad is a dish consisting of various kinds of fruit, sometimes served in a liquid, either their own juices or a syrup. In different forms, fruit salad can be served as an appetizer, a side salad, or a dessert.

Ingredients

  • Strawberries
  • Mangos
  • Kiwi
  • Blue berries
  • Pineapple
  • Whiped cream

Steps

  1. Cut fruit into large pieces
  2. Prepare whiped cream
  3. Merge above ingredients
  4. Stir until fruit is evenly covered
  5. Serve!
